# Scenario 8: AI Tools — Standard

## What It Is

Standard AI tools cover text translation, entity extraction (prebuilt and custom), and category classification (custom).

## When It's Used

- Translating customer messages in multilingual support scenarios
- Extracting entities (names, dates, addresses, product codes) from documents or messages
- Classifying support tickets into categories automatically
- Processing structured data from unstructured text inputs

## Credit Consumption

**15 Copilot Credits per 10 responses** (1.5 credits per response)

## Licensed vs. Unlicensed User Comparison

| User Type | Credits Per 10 Responses | Monthly Cost (10,000 responses) |
|-----------|--------------------------|--------------------------------|
| M365 Copilot Licensed User (B2E, internal channel) | 0 (included) | $0 |
| Unlicensed User | 15 credits per 10 responses | $150 |
| External User | 15 credits per 10 responses | $150 |

## Cost Comparison: PAYG vs. Capacity Pack

**Example — Multilingual Support Agent:** Translates 500 responses/day into 3 target languages = 1,500 translation responses/day = 45,000/month.

- Credits = (45,000 ÷ 10) × 15 = 67,500 credits/month

| Billing Model | Calculation | Monthly Cost | Annual Cost |
|---------------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| Pay-As-You-Go | 67,500 × $0.01 | $675 | $8,100 |
| Capacity Packs | 3 packs × $200 (75,000 credits) | $600 | $7,200 |
| 2 Packs + PAYG | $400 + (17,500 × $0.01 = $175) | $575 | $6,900 |

### Additional Example

**Entity Extraction Agent:** Extracts 5 entities per document from 200 documents/day = 1,000 extraction responses/day = 30,000/month. 
- Credits = 4,500/month = $45 PAYG.
