# Scenario 2: Generative Answer

## What It Is

Generative answers are dynamically generated by an AI model (such as GPT). The agent searches connected knowledge sources (SharePoint, uploaded documents, websites) and constructs a contextual response using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). They adapt to user queries and provide more natural, flexible interactions than classic answers.

## When It's Used

- Answering open-ended questions from knowledge bases (product manuals, HR handbooks, IT documentation)
- Troubleshooting queries requiring contextual reasoning
- Product support where responses vary by situation
- Onboarding guides that adapt to employee role or department
- Any scenario where static answers are insufficient

## Cost Comparison: PAYG vs. Capacity Pack

**Example:** A customer support agent answers product questions. Average interaction: 4 classic answers + 2 generative answers per session. Serves 900 customers/day (all unlicensed).

- Credits per session = (4 × 1) + (2 × 2) = 8 credits
- Daily total = 8 × 900 = 7,200 credits/day = 216,000 credits/month

| Billing Model | Calculation | Monthly Cost | Annual Cost |
|---------------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| Pay-As-You-Go | 216,000 × $0.01 | $2,160 | $25,920 |
| Capacity Packs | 9 packs × $200 (225,000 credits) | $1,800 | $21,600 |
| 8 Packs + PAYG | $1,600 + (16,000 × $0.01 = $160) | $1,760 | $21,120 |

### Additional Examples

**Example A — IT Knowledge Base Agent:** 200 employees ask 2 generative questions/day = 800 credits/day = 24,000/month. Pure PAYG costs $240/month. One capacity pack at $200 covers this and saves $40/month.

**Example B — Mixed Classic + Generative Agent:** An agent returns 3 classic greetings (3 credits) then 5 generative answers (10 credits) per session. At 500 sessions/day: 6,500 credits/day = 195,000/month. Eight capacity packs ($1,600) is cheaper than PAYG ($1,950).
